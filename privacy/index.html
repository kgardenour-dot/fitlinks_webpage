Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bandit Innovations collects and uses information through the FitLinks app.

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Information We Collect

FitLinks may collect the following information:

  • Account email address.
  • App usage information.
  • Content you enter, including saved links, titles, notes, and collections.

How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functionality.
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • To improve product quality and user experience.

Third-Party Services

FitLinks may use third-party services to operate and improve the app, including:

  • Supabase (backend/database and account services).
  • Apple and TestFlight (distribution, diagnostics, and app operations).
  • Expo services, where relevant to app delivery and maintenance.

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as needed to provide FitLinks and support users. To request data deletion, email help@fitlinks.app.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we work to keep your data protected.

Children's Privacy

FitLinks is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact help@fitlinks.app.
