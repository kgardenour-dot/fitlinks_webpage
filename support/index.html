Support

Need help with FitLinks? Email help@fitlinks.app.

Typical response time: 1-2 business days.

How to Report an Issue

  1. Your device model and iOS version.
  2. Your FitLinks app version/build.
  3. Exact steps to reproduce the issue.
  4. A screenshot or screen recording, if available.

Common Issues

  • Import/Share flow: if FitLinks is missing from Share options, scroll and tap Edit Actions to enable it.
  • Save failures: check internet connectivity, then try again after fully closing and reopening the app.
  • Collections behavior: if a link is not where expected, verify the selected collection before saving and refresh the app view.
